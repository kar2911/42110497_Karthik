Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is often called the "City of Light" for its rich history, culture, and art. Famous for iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum, it is a global center for fashion, gastronomy, and romance. Strolling along the Seine River or exploring its charming neighborhoods, like Montmartre and Le Marais, reveals its timeless charm. With its blend of history and modernity, Paris is a top destination for travelers worldwide.
