London

London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is a bustling metropolis blending rich history with modern innovation. Iconic landmarks like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the Houses of Parliament attract millions of visitors annually. Known for its world-class museums, theaters, and shopping districts like Oxford Street, it is also a hub for finance and global culture. From the historic Thames River to the vibrant markets of Camden, London offers something for everyone.
